    Offline QR cards are physical cards embedded with a unique QR code assigned to drivers or parents. These cards offer a convenient way to verify pickups without needing a mobile app or internet connection.

    When picking up a student, the driver or parent presents the QR card. School staff can then scan the QR code using the EZpick system to instantly verify the identity of the individual and confirm they are authorized for pickup.

    If you lose your card, please report it immediately to the school administration or deactivate the card from the mobile application. This way you can deactivate the lost card and issue a new one to ensure continued safety and security.

    Yes, Parents or schools can issue multiple QR cards for various family members or authorized individuals, ensuring flexibility in the pickup routine.

    Replacement policies may vary by school. Please check with your school's administration regarding any potential fees for replacement cards.

    Each QR card is encrypted with a unique code linked to the student's profile in the EZpick system. Only authorized school staff with the EZpick scanner can read and verify the cards, ensuring top-notch security.
